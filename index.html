What is RamenWar?

RAMEWAR is a role-playing game in bsc that will be based
on an ecosystem of NFT (non-fungible token), an interoperable game
piece that can be used in the gameplay such as tournament entry,
access to select skins, weapons, upgrades, and early access to the game.
RAMENWAR is an action role-playing game based on blockchain
technology.In this open virtual world, any player will be able to
collect NFT, fight, farm and explore all the environments of this vast
world. "

Decentralized ecosystem

The Decentralized Finance (Defi) game on Binance's smart chain, RAMENWAR is an innovative and self-sufficient RPG that offers the best gaming experiences and interfaces that allow players to have fun and get productive results at the same time.

Rewards

The game offers rewards through RAMEN and NFT tokens, which are financial in nature. The NFTs that the players receive, in addition to being a collection, will serve as props (for example, weapon / shield that will help in the performance of RAMENWAR players.

Open Source

The entire network and the game is open source. And players can own the data and also the deep part of how the code base and ecosystem evolve.Any programmer can contribute to the improvement of the game.

Nft´s

Ramenwar has a native token, RAMEN, which will be the bargaining chip to
acquire NFT, participate in the cultivation mechanisms and earn rewards.
The NFTs in RAMEN are not collectible cards, as in most current
blockchain games. Instead, these tokens are functional and can be used. Most of the items
in the game are tokenized: armor, weapons, mounts, all based on the foods that
make a ramen delicious. Become the best ramen master. Players can buy and sell
these NFTs on in-game auction houses or off-game NFT exchanges.
The in-game currency, RAMEN, can be bought and invested
in in-game businesses, such as taverns, shops. You can also farm for
rewards when you buy farmland, farms, or livestock and grow your own
rare vegetables.
